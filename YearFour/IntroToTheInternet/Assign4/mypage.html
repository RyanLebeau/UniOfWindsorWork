Ryan Lebeau's Day to Day

My name is Ryan Lebeau and I am a fourth year Honours Computer Science student. This webpage
will be about my day to day schedule and life since it can get pretty hectic. The average week starts
with an 8hour work day Monday at Chrysler (usually day shift). Then I try to do 2-3hours of work on
an Ignite project I have with Dr.Marquardt when I get home. The project is a Machine Learning model
around the seperations of proteins within LDL (a common measurement for heart attack prevention). On
Tuesday I have AI Concepts class and then my TA office hours for COMP-1400, which nobody ever goes to so it
gives me a chance to work on this class. Class begins at 10am Wednesday for Principle of Programming
Languages and then I am the Lab Instructor for a COMP-1400 lab. Thursday I have to work an afternoon shift
at Chrysler so I usually do as much school work as I can beforehand. Then, on Friday, I work days so
once I get home I finish up the 5hour week requirement for my Ignite project or any outstanding school
work I haven't completed. The weekend is a little bit more relaxed, with only one shift at Chrysler on saturday
and Sunday is a catch up day on school while watching the Lion's lose.

If you have any questions for me about a project I am working on please feel free to email me at lebeaur@uwindsor.ca

You are also wecome to visit my personal website but keep in mind it is a work in progress.
